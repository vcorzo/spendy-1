Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your username and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter for your transactions.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken, used solely to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly experience:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To identify bugs or performance issues and improve the Service.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial data to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

If you choose to delete your account, all associated data — including your expense records — will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days of the deletion request.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly currently does not integrate with third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may result in a network request to Google's servers when you visit the site. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle those requests.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app or via the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 5 business days.
